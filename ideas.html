Opportunities

Communities like Behance and Dribble provide a space for designers to share finished projects. There is often little discussion of process, concept or room to give feedback.

  1. Designers, thinkers and entrepreneurs need a way to give and receive feedback on in-process projects.

  2. People come up with unfinished ideas but don’t have the time, skills or motivation to complete the project on their own.

Users

The Builder: great craftmanship, great at a niche trade such as visual design or engineering, access to niche resources such as woodshops or 3D printers, needs inspiration for their next project.

The Thinker: has many conceptual ideas, but does not have the resources or skills to execute these ideas, needs collaborators to help make their ideas a reality.

The Economist: has knowledge of finance, supply chains and pricing, interested in getting involved in an entrepreneurial project, looking for ideas and teammates.

The Entrepreneur: great at collaboration and leadership, has some ideas but they are looking for feedback and teammates to help make their ideas realities.
